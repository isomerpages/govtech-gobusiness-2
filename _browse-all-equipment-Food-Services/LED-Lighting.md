## LED Lighting

### Technical Requirements

- LED self-ballasted lamps

- Direct LED replacements for compact fluorescent lamps (CFL) or linear fluorescent lamps (LFL)

- LED tube and strip lights

- LED circular tube

### Mode and Schedule of Payment 

- Direct Purchase
- Hire Purchase
- Lease

### Support Level and Conditions

Up to 70% for SMEs, 30% for non-SMEs

Applicants can apply for the EEG (Base Tier) through the [Business Grants Portal (BGP)](http://www.businessgrants.gov.sg/){:target="_blank"}{:rel="noopener"}
